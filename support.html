Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is DonateMe?

DonateMe is a Shopify app that helps merchants add donation functionality to their stores, allowing customers to make donations during checkout or through dedicated donation widgets.

How do I install DonateMe?

Install DonateMe from the Shopify App Store. After installation, the onboarding flow will guide you to set up your first donation product.

Is DonateMe compatible with my Shopify theme?

Yes. DonateMe works with all Shopify themes. Our donation widgets and progress bars adapt to your store's design.

Subscription Plans

What subscription plans are available?

DonateMe offers four plans: Free Plan, Bronze Plan, Silver Plan, and Gold Plan.

What are the limitations of the Free and Bronze plans?

Free and Bronze plans are limited to one donation product. For multiple donation products, upgrade to Silver or Gold.

How do I upgrade my subscription plan?

Go to the Plans tab in your DonateMe dashboard and select the plan that suits your needs. Follow the prompts to complete your upgrade.

Donation Products

What is a donation product?

A donation product is a special Shopify product created by DonateMe that allows customers to make donations. It can have a fixed price or allow customers to choose their donation amount.

How do I create a donation product?

Go to the Products tab in your DonateMe dashboard, click Create, enter a title, description, and minimum donation amount, then save your new donation product.

Can I limit how many donation products customers can add to cart?

Yes. Donation products are designed to allow only one quantity per cart to prevent confusion.

Why can't I create more than one donation product?

Free and Bronze plans are limited to one donation product. Upgrade to Silver or Gold for unlimited products.

Widgets and Display

How do I add a donation widget to my store?

Open the Shopify Theme Editor, click Add section or Add block, depending on your theme, select Donation Widget, and configure the widget settings and select your donation product.

How do I set up the donation progress bar?

Open the Shopify Theme Editor, add the Progress Bar Widget section or block, select your donation product and configure appearance settings, and set your fundraising goal in the product settings.

Can I customize the appearance of the donation widgets?

Yes. Both the donation widget and progress bar support customization including colors, font sizes, and button styles through the Shopify theme editor.

How do I remove the Powered by DonateMe branding?

Branding removal is available on Silver and Gold plans. In the theme editor, enable Remove Branding in the widget settings.

Donations and Orders

How are donations processed?

Donations are processed as regular Shopify orders. When a customer donates, it creates a special variant of your donation product with their chosen amount.

Do donations appear in my Shopify orders?

Yes. All donations appear as regular orders in your Shopify admin for easy tracking and management.

How do I set up automatic fulfillment for donations?

In the DonateMe Settings tab, toggle on Auto-Fulfill Orders. This automatically marks donation orders as fulfilled.

Can I apply sales tax to donations?

Yes. In Settings, toggle Apply Sales Tax on or off for your donation products.

Analytics and Tracking

How do I track donation progress?

The Reports tab shows real-time metrics including total donations and monthly donations.

Can I export donation data?

Yes. Export donation data from the Reports section of the dashboard.

Technical Support

How do I get support for DonateMe?

Contact our support team at support@donateme.app for assistance with any issues or questions.

Does DonateMe work with other Shopify apps?

DonateMe is designed to be compatible with most Shopify apps. If you encounter conflicts, contact support and we can help investigate.

Donation Progress Bar Setup Guide

Track your fundraising goals in real time with the Donation Progress Bar on your Shopify store.

Adding the Progress Bar

1. Go to Shopify Admin ‚Üí Online Store ‚Üí Themes.

Navigate to your Shopify admin panel and access your store's themes.

2. Click Customize on your active theme.

Select the theme customization option to modify your active theme.

3. From the left sidebar, click Add section.

Open the sidebar menu and look for the Add section option.

4.Select Donation Progress Bar.

Choose the Donation Progress Bar from the available section options.

5. The progress bar will appear in your theme preview.

The progress bar will now appear on your storefront preview

Configuring Your Progress Bar

  • Goal Amount: Set your fundraising target (e.g., $1,000) from the admin side of shopify.
  • Raised Amount: Automatically updates when donations are made.
  • Colors & Styling: Adjust bar color, background, and font style.
  • Placement: Add the section anywhere (homepage, cart, product page).

How It Works

  • Each donation updates the Raised value in real time.
  • The progress bar shows the percentage towards the Goal.
  • Once the goal is reached, it displays 100% Complete.

Example Use Case

  • Goal: $1,000
  • Raised: $0 (initially)
  • As customers donate, the raised amount increases and the bar fills up

‚öôÔ∏è Store Settings - Quick Guide

Configure your donation system settings to customize order processing, email notifications, and tagging features.

General Settings

  • Auto Fulfill Orders: Toggle ON/OFF to automatically mark donation orders as fulfilled.
    • Enable: For digital donations (no shipping needed)
    • Disable: For physical items requiring manual fulfillment
  • Sales Tax: Toggle ON/OFF to apply sales tax to donation products.
  • Shipping Requirements: Toggle ON/OFF to require a shipping address for donations.
    • Enable: Only if sending physical thank-you items
    • Disable: Recommended for most donations
  • Email Notifications: Toggle ON/OFF to send receipt emails to donors.
    • Recommended: Keep enabled for better donor experience

Order Tagging (Gold Plan Only)

Purpose: Automatically tag orders containing donations.

  • How to use: Enter a tag value (e.g., "donation", "charitable"). Orders with donations get tagged automatically for easy filtering in Shopify admin.
  • Free/Bronze Plans: This feature is unavailable. Upgrade to Gold to enable.

Email Configuration

  • From Email: Enter a professional sender email (e.g., donations@yourstore.com).
    • Must be valid email format
  • CC Email (Optional): Add multiple emails separated by commas.
    • Useful for admin notifications and record-keeping
    • No duplicates allowed

Email Template

  • Subject Line: Set a professional subject (e.g., "Thank you for your donation").
  • Template Editor: Customize the receipt email using the text editor.
  • Dynamic Variables:
    • {{donor_name}}: Customer name
    • {{date}}: Donation date
    • {{total}}: Donation amount
  • Example: 
    Dear {{donor_name}},
Thank you for your donation of {{total}} on {{date}}.
Best regards,
[Your Organization]

Saving Changes

  • Make changes to any settings. The "Save Settings" button becomes active when changes are detected.
  • Click "Save Settings" to apply all changes.
  • Validation: Fix any email format errors before saving.
  • Button states:
    • Gray (Disabled): No changes
    • Blue (Enabled): Ready to save
    • Loading: Saving in progress

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Email errors: Check format (e.g., user@domain.com), remove spaces, and avoid duplicates.
  • Not saving: Fix validation errors and check your internet connection.
  • No receipt emails: Enable notifications toggle and verify the "From" email.
Remember to save your settings after making changes!
